Privacy Policy 

Farkle Bot - Kingdom Come: Deliverance Discord Server 

Last Updated: June 16, 2026 



1 - Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how the Farkle Bot ("the Bot") collects, uses, stores, and protects information obtained through its operation within the Kingdom Come: Deliverance (KCD) Discord server. 

The Bot is a non-commercial, community-created project developed exclusively for entertainment purposes within the KCD community. It is not intended for commercial use, advertising, profiling, or data analytics. 

By interacting with the Bot, you acknowledge and accept the data practices described in this Privacy Policy. 


 
2 - Data We Collect

The Bot collects and stores only the minimum amount of information necessary to provide its gameplay and leaderboard functionality. 

The following data may be stored: 

    Discord User ID (a unique numerical identifier assigned by Discord). 

    Total games played. 

    Win/loss statistics associated with each player. 

The Bot does not retain or store: 

    Discord usernames or display names. 

    User profile information. 

    Message content unrelated to Bot commands. 

    Voice communications or media content. 

    Email addresses or other contact information. 

    IP addresses, device identifiers, or location data. 

    Personal information outside the Bot's gameplay functionality. 

The Bot may receive Discord-provided account information while processing commands, but only Discord User IDs and gameplay statistics are retained.  

Discord User IDs are retained as pseudonymous identifiers solely for associating gameplay records with the correct Discord account.  

The Bot only processes message content necessary to recognize and execute its own commands. It does not monitor, archive, analyze, or retain general conversations occurring within the Discord server. 



3 - Purpose and Legal Basis for Processing

The collected information is used solely for the following purposes: 

    Associating game results with individual players. 

    Maintaining persistent gameplay statistics. 

    Generating and displaying the Farkle leaderboard. 

    Operating and maintaining the Bot's core functionality. 

Data processing is limited to what is reasonably necessary for these purposes. The Bot does not use collected information for advertising, behavioral analysis, automated profiling, or any unrelated activity. 

Where applicable, processing is based on the legitimate interest of operating the Bot's game features and, on the user’s, voluntary interaction with the Bot. 


 
4 - Data Storage and Security 

Bot data is stored locally in a private data file hosted on a private home server located in the United States. Access to the host system and stored gameplay data is limited to the bot developer and the KCD Discord server administrator responsible for operating the Bot. 

Users understand that stored gameplay data may be processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in the user’s country.  

No gameplay data is stored in third-party cloud services or external analytics platforms. 

Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are used to protect stored information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure, including restricted access to the host system and private network controls. 

While no electronic storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure, reasonable efforts are made to safeguard the limited data maintained by the Bot. 


 
5 - Data Sharing and Disclosure 

The Bot does not sell, rent, license, trade, or otherwise share user data with third parties. 

Collected data is never disclosed to advertisers, marketing services, data brokers, or analytics providers. 

The only information that may be visible to other Discord users is gameplay-related leaderboard information, which is displayed within the KCD Discord server when the appropriate Bot command is used. By voluntarily participating in Bot games, users understand that their game statistics may appear on these community leaderboards. 

 

6 - Data Retention

Gameplay statistics are retained to preserve persistent player records and leaderboard functionality until the user requests deletion or the Bot is discontinued.  

Users may request deletion of their stored data at any time as described in Section 7. 

If the Bot is permanently discontinued, all locally stored user data associated with its operation will be permanently deleted within a reasonable period. 


 
7 - User Rights and Data Deletion 

Users may request to: 

    Obtain confirmation regarding whether data associated with their Discord User ID is stored. 

    Access the information stored about them. 

    Correct inaccurate information, where applicable. 

    Request permanent deletion of all stored data associated with their Discord User ID. 

    Withdraw from future participation by ceasing to use the Bot. 

Requests regarding privacy or data deletion may be submitted using the contact information provided in Section 10. 

Reasonable efforts will be made to process valid requests within 30 days of receipt. 


 
8 - Children's Privacy 

The Bot is not intended for use by children under the minimum age required by Discord's Terms of Service. The Bot does not knowingly collect information from users below the minimum age required by Discord's Terms of Service. 

If it is believed that a user under the applicable minimum age has interacted with the Bot and had data stored, the developer should be contacted so that the relevant information can be removed. 


 
9 - Scope of Operation 

The Farkle Bot is designed exclusively for use within the Kingdom Come: Deliverance Discord community and is not intended for general public distribution or commercial deployment. 

This Privacy Policy applies solely to interactions with the Bot and does not govern Discord itself or any third-party services operated independently of the Bot. 

Users should also review Discord's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service for information regarding Discord's handling of personal data.
